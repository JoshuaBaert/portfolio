About Me

Joshua Baert

Born and raised in Spanish Fork Utah, now living in Provo, Utah. I grew up around computers with my father being an IT Architect, working in the web industry, I generally fell in love with playing around with computers. I found a lot of enjoyment with playing with Linux systems while in junior high and high school.

After high school I worked as a network technician installing many types of low voltage cabling more specifically CAT5 cabling with networking closet hardware and other devices.

Deciding that I wanted more than working construction for my work I started going to school. As a side job I picked up work at a call center where I worked as technical support for QuickBooks Online and Online Payroll which gave me a solid foundation in accounting and accounting principles. Meanwhile working on my university degree in computer science.

Hoping to follow in my father's footsteps I am wanting to start my career as a web developer in hopes to more on to IT Architectural work.
